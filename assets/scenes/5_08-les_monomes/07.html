La division de notation exponentielle s’obtient par la soustraction des exposants.
Exemples :
a) (a^5)/(a^2) = a^(5-2) = a^3         a^5 divisé par a^2 égale a^3 parce que
(a^5)/(a^2) = ((a)(a)(a)(a)(a))/((a)(a)) = (a)(a)(a) = a^(1+1+1) = a^3
b) (b^3)/(b^2) = b^(3-2) = b^1 = b
c) (12^5)/(12^2) = 12^(5-2) = 12^3
d) (9^4)/(9^(-5)) = 9^(4-(-5)) = 9^(4+5) = 9^9
d) (a^(-6))/(a^(-3)) = a^((-6)-(-3)) = a^((-6)+3) = a^(-3)
